Project Overview

The Strategic Product Placement Analysis project aims to analyze the relationship between product positioning, pricing, promotion, and consumer behavior to identify key factors that influence sales performance.

Using Tableau, interactive visualizations, dashboards, and data stories were created to help understand how different product placement strategies impact sales and customer engagement. The project provides actionable insights that can help businesses optimize product positioning and increase revenue.

---

Project Objectives

- Analyze the impact of product placement on sales volume
- Evaluate the influence of pricing and competitor pricing
- Understand consumer demographics and purchasing behavior
- Measure the effect of promotion and seasonal factors
- Create interactive dashboards and stories using Tableau
- Provide data-driven recommendations

---

Dataset Description

The dataset contains retail product information including:

Field Name| Description
Product ID| Unique identifier for each product
Product Category| Category of the product
Product Position| Placement location (Shelf, Endcap, Aisle)
Price| Selling price of product
Competitor Price| Competitor's product price
Promotion| Promotion applied (Yes/No)
Foot Traffic| Number of customers passing product
Consumer Demographics| Customer type (Families, Seniors, Young Adults, College Students)
Seasonal| Seasonal or Non-seasonal product
Sales Volume| Number of units sold
